Project Status

Is Protractor actively maintained?

Although active development on Protractor ended in December of 2022, the project will continue to be supported under Long Term Support and will continue to receive critical security & compatibility fixes via the community through August 2023. This is important as there are many actively developed projects still benefiting from their existing code coverage, and we anticipate Protractor to continue functioning well into the future.

In short: Protractor's current status is Long Term Support and will End Of Life in September 2023.

What does End Of Life mean?

The packages will remain accessible on GitHub, npm, Bower, and Release archive. This website will also remain here indefinitely. The project will be archived on GitHub and remain accessible indefinitely, but will no longer be open to issues / comments / etc.

Support After August 2023

For those who need continued support after August 2023, Google has partnered with HeroDevs to secure free access to their Never Ending Support For Protractor product for the entire community through August 2024, after which commercial licenses will be available to those who continue to depend on the value that existing Protractor tests provide.

See our State of end-to-end Testing with Angular post to learn more.
